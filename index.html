At The AI Table, leaders from industry, government, and academia unite to shape the future of artificial intelligence. We explore AI strategy, governance, and societal impact—covering innovation, risks, governance, policy, and workforce transformation.

AI Reports & Policy Briefs

We author reports and policy briefs, grounded in rigorous analysis and sound data. These resources offer practical recommendations for trailblazers looking to effectively govern AI. We emphasize tackling the application of AI in ways that minimize or eliminate harm. Our findings can be used to inform important governance, public policy, and corporate investment decisions.

AI Resource Library

Our AI Resource Library is the world's most comprehensive inventory of AI use cases, training, frameworks, policies, and best practice materials — all housed in one centralized, well-organized location. It's designed to support AI decision-makers and practitioners in confidently navigating challenges and applying AI with forethought, skill, and precision.
